‚öñÔ∏è Balance is Key

Healthy gaming means maintaining balance in all areas of your life:

  • Physical Health: Take regular breaks to stand, stretch, and rest your eyes. Stay hydrated.
  • Sleep Schedule: Don't sacrifice sleep for gaming. Our limited hours (10 PM - 2 AM) help, but set your own bedtime.
  • Responsibilities First: Complete work, school, and household tasks before logging in to play.
  • Social Connections: Gaming can be social, but don't let it replace in-person friendships and family time.
  • Diverse Activities: Balance gaming with exercise, hobbies, reading, outdoor activities, and other interests.

üö® Warning Signs to Watch For

While Moe's Card Room doesn't involve real money, gaming can still become problematic if it:

  • Interferes with work, school, or personal relationships
  • Causes you to neglect responsibilities or self-care
  • Becomes your primary way to cope with stress or negative emotions
  • Feels compulsive or out of your control
  • Leads to irritability or anxiety when you can't play

If you recognize these signs, consider taking a break, talking to someone you trust, or seeking professional support.
